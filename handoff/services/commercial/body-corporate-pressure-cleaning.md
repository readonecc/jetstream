## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our body corporate pressure cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: Do you work with strata managers and body corporate committees?
**A1**: Yes. We work directly with strata managers, body corporate committees, and property management companies across Brisbane. We provide detailed quotes, scheduled cleaning plans, and all necessary documentation including insurance certificates and licensing details.

**Q2**: How often should common areas be cleaned?
**A2**: We recommend pressure cleaning shared common areas every 6 to 12 months, depending on foot traffic, shade coverage, and how quickly mould and algae build up. Properties surrounded by trees or in high-humidity areas may benefit from more frequent cleaning.

**Q3**: Can you provide a maintenance schedule?
**A3**: Absolutely. We offer tailored maintenance schedules for body corporate properties so your common areas are cleaned regularly without you having to manage individual bookings. We'll work with your committee to set a schedule that suits your property and budget.

**Q4**: Are you insured for body corporate work?
**A4**: Yes. We carry $20 million in public liability insurance and provide all licensing and insurance documentation upfront with every quote. This gives your committee and strata manager complete peace of mind before any work begins.

**Q5**: What areas do you clean?
**A5**: We clean all shared areas including walkways, entryways, car parks, driveways, courtyards, bin areas, and outdoor communal spaces. If it's a shared surface within your strata property, we can clean it.
