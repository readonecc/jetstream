## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our graffiti removal service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: Can you remove graffiti from brick walls?
**A1**: Yes. Brick is one of the most common surfaces we treat for graffiti. We use specialised cleaning solutions and pressure cleaning techniques to remove spray paint from brick without damaging the mortar or surface texture.

**Q2**: How quickly can you respond to graffiti?
**A2**: We aim to respond as quickly as possible. In most cases, we can provide a same-day quote and schedule the removal promptly. Fast removal is important — it reduces the chance of repeat vandalism and prevents the paint from setting deeper into the surface.

**Q3**: Will removal damage my wall surface?
**A3**: No. We tailor our approach to the surface type and use the appropriate pressure levels and cleaning solutions to remove the graffiti without causing damage. Our goal is to restore your wall to its original condition.

**Q4**: Can you remove graffiti from painted surfaces?
**A4**: Yes. We can remove graffiti from painted walls, though results vary depending on the type of paint and the condition of the existing surface. We assess each job individually and advise on the expected outcome before starting.

**Q5**: Do you offer anti-graffiti coatings?
**A5**: We can advise on anti-graffiti coating options that make future removal faster and easier. These protective coatings create a barrier that prevents spray paint from bonding to the surface, allowing it to be washed off more readily if vandalism occurs again.
