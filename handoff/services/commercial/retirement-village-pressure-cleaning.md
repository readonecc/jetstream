## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our retirement village pressure cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: Do you have experience with retirement villages?
**A1**: Yes. We've worked with retirement villages and aged care communities across Brisbane. We understand the importance of safety, minimal disruption, and thorough cleaning in environments where elderly residents live and move around daily.

**Q2**: How do you minimise disruption to residents?
**A2**: We schedule our work at times that suit the village management and residents. We section off areas as we clean to maintain safe access, keep noise to reasonable levels, and ensure walkways are safe to use as soon as each section is completed.

**Q3**: Can you provide a regular maintenance schedule?
**A3**: Absolutely. We offer tailored maintenance schedules for retirement villages so shared areas are cleaned regularly without management having to coordinate individual bookings. We'll work with your facility manager to set a cleaning frequency that suits your community's needs.

**Q4**: Do you clean courtyards and seating areas?
**A4**: Yes. We clean all shared outdoor areas including courtyards, seating areas, gazebos, barbecue surrounds, and any other communal spaces within the village grounds. These areas benefit greatly from regular cleaning to stay safe and inviting for residents.

**Q5**: Are your staff trained for aged care environments?
**A5**: Our team understands the care and consideration required when working in retirement and aged care settings. We take extra precautions to ensure resident safety, maintain clear communication with village management, and work in a way that respects the living environment of all residents.
