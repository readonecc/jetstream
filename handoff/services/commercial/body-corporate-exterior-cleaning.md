- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our body corporate exterior cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: What does body corporate exterior cleaning include?
**A1**: Our body corporate exterior cleaning service covers shared pathways, walkways, entryways, building surrounds, courtyards, driveways, and communal outdoor areas. We remove mould, algae, dirt, grime, and environmental staining from all accessible shared surfaces.

**Q2**: Do you clean building facades?
**A2**: Yes. We clean exterior building walls and facades as part of our body corporate exterior cleaning service. We assess each surface and use the appropriate pressure and cleaning method to deliver a thorough clean without causing damage.

**Q3**: Can you clean multi-storey buildings?
**A3**: We service a range of strata property sizes. For multi-storey buildings, we assess the job on-site and use the appropriate equipment to safely and effectively clean all accessible exterior surfaces.

**Q4**: How often should strata exteriors be cleaned?
**A4**: We recommend exterior cleaning every 6 to 12 months depending on the property's location, exposure to weather, and the rate of mould and algae growth. Properties in shaded or high-humidity areas across Brisbane may benefit from more frequent cleaning.

**Q5**: Do you provide reports for body corporate committees?
**A5**: Yes. We can provide before-and-after documentation and completion reports for body corporate committees and strata managers. This makes it easy to demonstrate the work completed and justify maintenance spending at committee meetings.
