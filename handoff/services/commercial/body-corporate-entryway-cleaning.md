## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our body corporate entryway cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: What does entryway cleaning include?
**A1**: Our entryway cleaning service covers building entrances, shared entry paths, entry plazas, and front building surrounds. We remove dirt, mould, algae, and environmental staining from all accessible entry surfaces to keep your property looking clean and welcoming.

**Q2**: How often should entryways be cleaned?
**A2**: We recommend cleaning shared entryways every 6 to 12 months depending on foot traffic and exposure to weather. High-traffic entrances in larger complexes may benefit from quarterly cleaning to maintain a consistently clean appearance.

**Q3**: Do you clean lobby areas?
**A3**: Our service focuses on exterior entryway surfaces including paths, surrounds, and building frontage. For interior lobby areas, we recommend speaking with us about what's included so we can tailor a solution to your property's specific needs.

**Q4**: Can you clean entry stairs and ramps?
**A4**: Yes. We clean entry stairs, ramps, and all associated surfaces around building entrances. These areas are particularly important to keep clean as mould and algae buildup can create slip hazards for residents and visitors.

**Q5**: Do you work outside resident peak hours?
**A5**: Yes. We understand that entryways are high-traffic areas, so we're happy to schedule cleaning early in the morning, on weekends, or at other times that minimise disruption to residents across Brisbane strata properties.
