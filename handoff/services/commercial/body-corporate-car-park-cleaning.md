## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our body corporate car park cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: Can you clean basement car parks?
**A1**: Yes. We clean both basement and outdoor car parks in strata complexes. For basement car parks, we assess ventilation and drainage requirements before starting to ensure a safe and effective clean.

**Q2**: How do you manage resident access during cleaning?
**A2**: We work with your strata manager or body corporate committee to schedule cleaning at times that minimise disruption. For larger car parks, we can clean in sections so residents always have access to parking during the process.

**Q3**: Do you remove oil stains?
**A3**: Yes. Oil stains are one of the most common issues we treat in body corporate car parks. Our professional-grade equipment and cleaning solutions break down oil and grease buildup, restoring the surface to a clean condition.

**Q4**: How often should body corporate car parks be cleaned?
**A4**: We recommend cleaning shared car parks every 6 to 12 months depending on usage levels and the rate of oil and grime buildup. High-traffic car parks in larger complexes across Brisbane may benefit from quarterly cleaning.

**Q5**: Can you clean parking ramps?
**A5**: Yes. We clean parking ramps, access lanes, and all associated surfaces within your strata car park. Ramps in particular benefit from regular cleaning as they can become slippery when mould or oil accumulates on the surface.
