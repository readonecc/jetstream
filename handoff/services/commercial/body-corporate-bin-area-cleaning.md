## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our body corporate bin area cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: How often should bin areas be cleaned?
**A1**: We recommend cleaning shared bin areas every 1 to 3 months depending on the size of the complex and how heavily the waste area is used. Regular cleaning prevents buildup from becoming a hygiene issue and helps manage odours throughout the year.

**Q2**: Do you remove odours as well?
**A2**: Yes. Our pressure cleaning process removes the waste residue and organic matter that causes odours in bin storage areas. By eliminating the source of the smell rather than masking it, the result lasts until new buildup occurs — which is why regular cleaning is so effective.

**Q3**: Can you clean on a regular schedule?
**A3**: Absolutely. We offer scheduled maintenance plans for body corporate bin areas so your waste disposal zones are cleaned regularly without you having to manage individual bookings. We'll work with your strata manager to set a frequency that suits your property.

**Q4**: Do you clean around the bins or just the pad?
**A4**: We clean the entire bin area including the pad, surrounding surfaces, walls, and any accessible areas where waste residue, grease, or grime has accumulated. We leave the whole area clean, not just the floor.

**Q5**: Is this service available for residential properties too?
**A5**: Yes. While this page focuses on body corporate and strata properties, we also offer bin pad and waste area cleaning for standalone residential properties across Brisbane. Get in touch for a quote tailored to your needs.
