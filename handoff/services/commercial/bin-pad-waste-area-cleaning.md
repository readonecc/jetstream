## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our bin pad and waste area cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: How often should bin areas be cleaned?
**A1**: We recommend bin pad cleaning every 1 to 3 months depending on usage and the type of waste being handled. Restaurants, food outlets, and high-occupancy apartment complexes may benefit from monthly cleaning to stay on top of odours and hygiene.

**Q2**: Do you remove odours?
**A2**: Yes. Our pressure cleaning process removes the grease, residue, and organic matter that cause bad odours. By eliminating the source of the smell rather than masking it, your waste areas stay fresher for longer.

**Q3**: Can you clean dumpster surrounds?
**A3**: Absolutely. We clean the full bin pad area including the concrete pad, surrounding surfaces, and areas around dumpsters and bins. The goal is to leave the entire waste zone clean and hygienic.

**Q4**: Is this service available for restaurants?
**A4**: Yes. Restaurants and food outlets are among the most common clients for this service. We understand the hygiene demands of the food industry and schedule cleans to suit your operating hours.

**Q5**: Do you clean both residential and commercial waste areas?
**A5**: Yes. We service bin pads and waste areas for both commercial properties and residential complexes including apartment buildings and strata properties. The same professional standard applies regardless of the property type.
