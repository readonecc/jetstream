## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our car park pressure cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: Can you clean basement car parks?
**A1**: Yes. We clean both open and basement car parks. For enclosed or underground parking areas, we use equipment suited to the space and ensure proper drainage management throughout the cleaning process.

**Q2**: How do you handle oil stains?
**A2**: We pre-treat oil stains with professional-grade degreasing solutions before pressure cleaning. This breaks down the oil so it can be effectively removed during the wash, leaving your car park surface clean and stain-free.

**Q3**: Can you work outside business hours?
**A3**: Absolutely. We understand that car parks are in constant use during the day. We're happy to schedule cleaning after hours, overnight, or on weekends to minimise disruption to tenants, customers, and visitors.

**Q4**: How often should car parks be cleaned?
**A4**: We recommend professional car park cleaning every 3 to 6 months depending on traffic volume and usage. High-traffic commercial car parks or food-adjacent parking areas may benefit from more frequent cleans.

**Q5**: Do you clean line markings as well?
**A5**: Our pressure cleaning service cleans around and over existing line markings. We take care not to damage them, though heavily worn markings may become more visible once the surrounding surface is cleaned. We can advise if repainting is recommended.
