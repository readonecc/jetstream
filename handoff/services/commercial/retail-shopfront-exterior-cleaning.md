## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our retail shopfront exterior cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: How often should shopfronts be cleaned?
**A1**: We recommend cleaning retail shopfronts every 3 to 6 months depending on foot traffic, location, and exposure to environmental elements. Shops in high-traffic areas or near busy roads may benefit from more frequent cleaning.

**Q2**: Can you work outside trading hours?
**A2**: Yes. We understand that disruption during business hours isn't ideal. We're happy to schedule cleaning early mornings, after close, or on weekends to keep your business running without interruption.

**Q3**: Do you clean awnings and signage?
**A3**: We clean exterior awning surfaces and surrounding areas as part of our shopfront service. For signage, we assess the material and condition to ensure safe cleaning without damage. Let us know what's included and we'll tailor the service to suit.

**Q4**: Is pressure washing safe for painted surfaces?
**A4**: Yes. We adjust pressure levels based on the surface being cleaned. For painted or delicate exterior finishes, we use lower pressure combined with professional cleaning solutions to deliver a thorough clean without causing damage.

**Q5**: Do you offer regular maintenance plans?
**A5**: We do. Many of our retail clients book recurring cleans on a monthly or quarterly basis. Regular maintenance keeps your shopfront consistently clean and saves you from dealing with heavy buildup between cleans.
