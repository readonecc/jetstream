## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our retail shopfront window cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: How often should shopfront windows be cleaned?
**A1**: We recommend cleaning shopfront windows every 2 to 4 weeks for most retail businesses. High-traffic locations or shops near busy roads may benefit from weekly cleaning to keep glass looking its best.

**Q2**: Do you clean window frames too?
**A2**: Yes. Our shopfront window cleaning includes the glass, edges, and surrounding frames and trims. We clean the full window area so everything looks consistent and well-maintained.

**Q3**: Can you clean large display windows?
**A3**: Absolutely. We clean display windows of all sizes, from standard shopfront glass to large floor-to-ceiling display panels. We use the right tools and techniques for each window type to ensure a streak-free result.

**Q4**: Is the finish streak-free?
**A4**: Yes. A streak-free finish is standard with our shopfront window cleaning service. We use professional-grade cleaning solutions and techniques to ensure your glass is left crystal clear with no streaks, smudges, or residue.

**Q5**: Do you offer regular cleaning schedules?
**A5**: We do. Many of our retail clients book recurring window cleans on a weekly, fortnightly, or monthly basis. Regular scheduling keeps your windows consistently clean and ensures your storefront always looks its best.
