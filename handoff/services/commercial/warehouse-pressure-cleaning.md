## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our warehouse pressure cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: Can you clean during business hours?
**A1**: Yes, we can work around your operating schedule. We're also happy to clean outside business hours, on weekends, or during shutdowns to avoid disrupting your daily operations.

**Q2**: How long does warehouse cleaning take?
**A2**: The timeframe depends on the size of your warehouse and the level of buildup. Most jobs are completed within a single day. We'll provide a clear estimate when we quote the job.

**Q3**: Do you handle grease and oil removal?
**A3**: Absolutely. Grease and oil are among the most common issues we deal with in warehouse environments. We use professional-grade equipment and pre-treatment methods to break down and remove even heavy grease buildup.

**Q4**: Can you clean loading bay areas?
**A4**: Yes. Loading bays are a key part of our warehouse cleaning service. We clean the full floor area including loading zones, access ramps, and surrounding surfaces.

**Q5**: How often should warehouses be cleaned?
**A5**: We recommend professional warehouse cleaning every 3 to 6 months depending on the level of traffic and the type of operations. High-traffic or food-handling facilities may benefit from more frequent cleans.
