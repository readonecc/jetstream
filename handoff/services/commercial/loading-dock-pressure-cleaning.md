## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our loading dock pressure cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: How often should loading docks be cleaned?
**A1**: We recommend cleaning loading docks every 1 to 3 months depending on traffic volume and the type of goods being handled. High-traffic docks or those dealing with food and liquids may need more frequent cleaning.

**Q2**: Can you work around delivery schedules?
**A2**: Absolutely. We understand loading docks are busy areas. We're happy to schedule cleaning outside delivery windows, after hours, or on weekends to avoid disrupting your operations.

**Q3**: Do you remove oil and grease stains?
**A3**: Yes. Oil and grease removal is a core part of our loading dock cleaning service. We use professional-grade equipment and pre-treatment methods to break down and remove even heavy, ground-in stains.

**Q4**: How long does the cleaning take?
**A4**: Most loading dock cleaning jobs are completed within a few hours depending on the size of the area and the level of buildup. We'll give you a clear timeframe when we provide your quote.

**Q5**: Do you clean access ramps too?
**A5**: Yes. Our loading dock service includes cleaning of surrounding access ramps, delivery bays, and entry points. We clean the full area to ensure a consistent result.
