## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our body corporate walkway cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: How often should body corporate walkways be cleaned?
**A1**: We recommend cleaning shared walkways every 6 to 12 months depending on foot traffic, shade coverage, and how quickly mould and algae return. Properties in shaded or high-humidity areas may need more frequent cleaning to stay safe and presentable.

**Q2**: Do you clean covered walkways?
**A2**: Yes. Covered walkways often accumulate more mould and algae because they receive less direct sunlight. We clean both covered and uncovered walkways using the appropriate pressure and cleaning methods for each surface.

**Q3**: Can you remove black algae staining?
**A3**: Yes. Black algae is one of the most common issues we treat on shared walkways across Brisbane. Our pressure cleaning equipment and professional-grade cleaning solutions remove black algae staining and restore the surface to a clean, safe condition.

**Q4**: Do you work with strata managers directly?
**A4**: Absolutely. We work directly with strata managers, body corporate committees, and property management companies. We provide detailed quotes, scheduled maintenance plans, and all necessary documentation including insurance certificates.

**Q5**: Is there a minimum area for this service?
**A5**: We don't impose a strict minimum, but we do recommend combining walkway cleaning with other shared areas to get the best value from each visit. We're happy to assess your property and provide a tailored quote based on the scope of work.
