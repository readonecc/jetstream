- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our commercial building washing service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: What types of commercial buildings do you clean?
**A1**: We clean a wide range of commercial properties including office buildings, retail centres, industrial facilities, and multi-tenancy complexes. Whether it's a single-storey shopfront or a larger commercial property, we have the equipment and experience to handle the job.

**Q2**: Can you work outside business hours?
**A2**: Yes. We understand that commercial properties need to remain operational during the day. We're happy to schedule cleaning outside business hours, on weekends, or early mornings to minimise disruption to your tenants and customers.

**Q3**: How often should commercial buildings be washed?
**A3**: We recommend commercial building washing every 6 to 12 months depending on the location, exposure to traffic, and environmental conditions. Properties in high-traffic or industrial areas may benefit from more frequent cleaning.

**Q4**: Are you insured for commercial work?
**A4**: Absolutely. We carry $20 million in public liability insurance and provide all licensing and insurance documentation upfront with every quote. You'll have complete peace of mind before we start any work on your property.

**Q5**: Do you clean multi-storey buildings?
**A5**: We service a range of commercial building sizes. For multi-storey properties, we assess the job on-site and use the appropriate equipment to safely and effectively clean all accessible exterior surfaces.
