## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our driveway painting and coating service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: What colours are available?
**A1**: We offer a range of exterior concrete coating colours to suit your property. During the quoting process, we'll discuss colour options and help you choose a finish that complements your home or building. Contact us to find out what's currently available.

**Q2**: How long does a driveway coating last?
**A2**: A professionally applied driveway coating typically lasts between 3 to 7 years depending on traffic levels, weather exposure, and the condition of the underlying concrete. We use professional-grade products designed to withstand Brisbane's climate and regular vehicle use.

**Q3**: Do you pressure clean before painting?
**A3**: Yes. Every driveway painting job includes a full pressure clean before any coating is applied. This removes dirt, mould, oil stains, and loose material so the coating bonds properly and delivers a long-lasting finish.

**Q4**: How long before I can drive on it?
**A4**: Curing times vary depending on the product used and weather conditions, but most driveway coatings can handle foot traffic within 24 hours and vehicle traffic within 48 to 72 hours. We'll provide specific curing guidance after every job.

**Q5**: Can you coat a paved driveway?
**A5**: It depends on the type of pavers and their condition. Some paved surfaces are suitable for exterior coatings, while others may require a different approach. Get in touch for a free assessment and we'll recommend the best option for your driveway.
