## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our walkway clear coat sealing service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: What is walkway clear coat sealing?
**A1**: Walkway clear coat sealing involves applying a transparent protective sealant over concrete pathways. It creates a durable barrier that prevents staining, resists moisture penetration, and protects against UV damage — all while enhancing the natural look of your walkway surface.

**Q2**: How long does the sealant last?
**A2**: A professionally applied clear coat sealant on walkways typically lasts between 2 to 5 years depending on foot traffic levels, weather exposure, and the condition of the concrete. We'll give you a realistic timeframe based on your specific walkway during the quoting process.

**Q3**: Do you clean the walkway first?
**A3**: Yes. Every walkway sealing job starts with a thorough clean to remove dirt, mould, and contaminants. This ensures the sealant bonds properly to the surface and delivers the longest-lasting protection possible.

**Q4**: Can you seal garden paths?
**A4**: Yes. We seal a range of pathway types including garden paths, entry walkways, and shared access paths. As long as the surface is suitable for a clear coat sealant, we can protect it. Contact us for a free assessment if you're unsure.

**Q5**: How soon can the walkway be used after sealing?
**A5**: Most walkways can handle light foot traffic within 24 hours of sealing. Full curing typically takes 48 to 72 hours depending on the product used and weather conditions. We'll provide specific curing guidance after every job.
