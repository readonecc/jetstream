## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our driveway anti-slip coating service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: What is a crushed glass anti-slip system?
**A1**: A crushed glass anti-slip system uses fine glass particles broadcast into a base coating to create a textured, slip-resistant surface. A protective top coat is then applied over the top to lock everything in place. The result is a durable, high-traction finish that significantly reduces the risk of slipping — even in wet conditions.

**Q2**: Is it safe for tyres?
**A2**: Yes. The crushed glass particles are fine enough to provide excellent grip without causing any damage to vehicle tyres. The system is specifically designed for driveways and parking areas that handle regular vehicle traffic.

**Q3**: How long does anti-slip coating last?
**A3**: A professionally applied anti-slip coating typically lasts between 3 to 7 years depending on traffic levels, weather exposure, and the slope of the driveway. We use professional-grade products formulated to withstand Brisbane's climate and daily use.

**Q4**: Can it be applied to sloped driveways?
**A4**: Absolutely. Sloped driveways are one of the most common applications for anti-slip coatings. The crushed glass system is particularly effective on inclines where standard concrete becomes dangerously slippery when wet.

**Q5**: Do you clean the driveway before applying the coating?
**A5**: Yes. Every anti-slip coating job starts with a full pressure clean to remove dirt, mould, oil, and loose material. This ensures the base coating bonds properly to the concrete and the finished system performs as expected for years to come.
