## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our walkway painting and coating service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: What colours are available for walkway coatings?
**A1**: We offer a range of exterior coating colours suitable for walkways and pathways. During the quoting process, we'll discuss your options and help you choose a colour that complements your property. Contact us to find out what's currently available.

**Q2**: How long does the coating last?
**A2**: A professionally applied walkway coating typically lasts between 3 to 7 years depending on foot traffic levels, weather exposure, and the condition of the concrete underneath. We use professional-grade products designed to withstand Brisbane's climate.

**Q3**: Do you clean the walkway before coating?
**A3**: Yes. Every walkway coating job includes a thorough clean before any coating is applied. This removes dirt, mould, and loose material so the coating bonds properly and delivers a long-lasting, even finish.

**Q4**: Can you coat over existing paint?
**A4**: In many cases, yes. It depends on the condition of the existing coating and the type of product that was previously applied. We'll assess your walkway during the quoting process and let you know whether the existing surface needs stripping or whether we can coat directly over it.

**Q5**: How soon can the walkway be used after coating?
**A5**: Most walkway coatings can handle light foot traffic within 24 hours. Full curing typically takes 48 to 72 hours depending on the product and weather conditions. We'll provide specific curing guidance after every job.
