## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our concrete sealing service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: What is concrete sealing?
**A1**: Concrete sealing involves applying a protective sealant over your concrete surfaces. It enhances the natural appearance while creating a durable barrier against staining, moisture penetration, and UV damage. The result is concrete that stays cleaner for longer and is protected against Brisbane's harsh weather conditions.

**Q2**: How long does concrete sealing last?
**A2**: A professionally applied sealant typically lasts between 2 to 5 years depending on traffic levels, weather exposure, and general wear. We'll advise you on the expected lifespan based on your specific surfaces and conditions during the quoting process.

**Q3**: Do you clean the concrete before sealing?
**A3**: Yes. Every concrete sealing job includes a full pressure clean before the sealant is applied. This removes dirt, mould, oil stains, and contaminants so the sealant bonds properly and delivers the best possible result.

**Q4**: What types of concrete can be sealed?
**A4**: We seal all types of concrete surfaces including plain concrete, exposed aggregate, stamped concrete, and coloured concrete. Whether it's a driveway, pathway, patio, or pool surround, we'll recommend the right sealant for your specific surface and conditions.

**Q5**: How long before I can walk or drive on sealed concrete?
**A5**: Curing times vary depending on the product used and weather conditions, but most surfaces can handle foot traffic within 24 hours and vehicle traffic within 48 to 72 hours. We'll provide specific curing guidance after every job.
