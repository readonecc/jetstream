## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our concrete painting service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: What is concrete painting?
**A1**: Concrete painting involves applying a durable exterior coating to your concrete surfaces. It restores worn, faded, or damaged-looking concrete with a fresh, uniform finish that enhances appearance and provides long-lasting protection against weather, UV exposure, and general wear.

**Q2**: How long does concrete painting last?
**A2**: A professionally applied exterior coating typically lasts between 3 to 7 years depending on traffic levels, weather exposure, and the condition of the underlying concrete. We'll advise you on the expected lifespan based on your specific surfaces during the quoting process.

**Q3**: What colours are available?
**A3**: We offer a range of colours suitable for exterior concrete surfaces. During the quoting process, we'll discuss colour options that complement your property and suit the specific application — whether it's a driveway, pathway, patio, or pool surround.

**Q4**: Do you repair cracks before painting?
**A4**: We assess the condition of your concrete before coating and address minor surface preparation as part of the job. For significant structural damage or deep cracking, we'll advise you on the best course of action before proceeding to ensure the coating adheres properly and lasts as long as possible.

**Q5**: Can you paint over previously coated concrete?
**A5**: In many cases, yes. We assess the condition of the existing coating and surface before recommending the best approach. If the previous coating is in reasonable condition, we can prepare the surface and apply a fresh coat. If it's peeling or failing, we'll remove it first to ensure proper adhesion.
