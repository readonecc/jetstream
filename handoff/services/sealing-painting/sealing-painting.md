## Section 5: Category Overview

- **Kicker**: WHY SEAL OR PAINT YOUR CONCRETE
- **H2**: Protect Your Investment With Professional Concrete Sealing & Painting

Brisbane's climate is tough on exposed concrete. Between the intense UV, heavy summer rain, humidity, and everyday wear, driveways and walkways break down faster than most homeowners expect. Mould takes hold, stains set in, and the surface gradually deteriorates. Professional sealing and painting is the most effective way to stop this cycle and keep your concrete looking and performing at its best for years to come.

We offer two main approaches depending on your goals. Clear coat sealing preserves the natural look of your concrete while adding a durable protective layer that resists moisture, oil, and UV damage. Coloured coatings and paints, on the other hand, let you completely transform the appearance of your driveway or walkway — covering old stains, refreshing faded surfaces, and creating a clean, uniform finish. For properties where safety is a priority, we also offer anti-slip coatings with a textured finish for added grip in wet conditions.

Every sealing and painting job at Jetstream starts with a thorough pressure clean. We remove all dirt, mould, oil, and debris before any coating goes down, because a clean surface is essential for proper adhesion and a long-lasting result. It is a clean-seal-protect workflow that delivers the best outcome every time — and right now, the clean is included free when you seal or paint.

## Section 13: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our sealing and painting services in Brisbane.

**FAQ 1**:
- **Q**: What is the difference between sealing and painting a driveway?
- **A**: Sealing applies a clear protective coat over your existing concrete, preserving its natural appearance while shielding it from moisture, UV, oil, and staining. Painting (or coating) adds a coloured layer that completely changes the look of the surface, covering imperfections and giving it a fresh, uniform finish. Both options protect your concrete — the choice comes down to whether you want to keep the natural look or transform it with colour.

**FAQ 2**:
- **Q**: How long does concrete sealing or painting last?
- **A**: A quality clear coat seal typically lasts between 3 and 7 years depending on traffic, sun exposure, and the type of sealant used. Coloured coatings generally last 5 to 10 years with proper application and preparation. We always start with a thorough clean to ensure the best adhesion and the longest-lasting result.

**FAQ 3**:
- **Q**: Do you clean the surface before sealing or painting?
- **A**: Absolutely. Every sealing and painting job includes a full pressure clean beforehand. Removing all dirt, mould, oil, and debris is essential — without a properly cleaned surface, coatings will not adhere correctly and will not last. Right now, the clean is included free when you seal or paint with us.

**FAQ 4**:
- **Q**: Do you offer anti-slip coatings for driveways?
- **A**: Yes. We offer anti-slip coatings with a textured finish that provides extra grip in wet conditions. This is especially popular for steep driveways, commercial properties, and homes with elderly residents. Anti-slip coatings are available in a range of colours and can be applied to both driveways and walkways.

**FAQ 5**:
- **Q**: How long does a sealed or painted driveway take to cure?
- **A**: Curing times vary depending on the product used and weather conditions, but most sealants and coatings require 24 to 48 hours before foot traffic and 72 hours before vehicle traffic. We will provide specific guidance for your job and advise on the best time to schedule to ensure optimal drying conditions.
