## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our driveway clear coat sealing service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: What is clear coat sealing?
**A1**: Clear coat sealing involves applying a transparent protective sealant over your concrete driveway. It enhances the natural appearance of the surface while creating a durable barrier against staining, moisture penetration, and UV damage. The result is a cleaner-looking driveway that stays protected for longer.

**Q2**: How long does driveway sealing last?
**A2**: A professionally applied clear coat sealant typically lasts between 2 to 5 years depending on traffic levels, weather exposure, and general wear. We'll advise you on the expected lifespan based on your specific driveway and conditions during the quoting process.

**Q3**: Do you clean the driveway before sealing?
**A3**: Yes. Every driveway sealing job includes a full pressure clean before the sealant is applied. This removes dirt, mould, oil stains, and contaminants so the sealant bonds properly and delivers the best possible result.

**Q4**: How long before I can drive on it?
**A4**: Curing times vary depending on the product used and weather conditions, but most driveways can handle foot traffic within 24 hours and vehicle traffic within 48 to 72 hours. We'll provide specific curing guidance after every job.

**Q5**: Can you seal a paved driveway?
**A5**: It depends on the type of pavers and their condition. Some paved surfaces are suitable for clear coat sealing, while others may require a different approach. Contact us for a free assessment and we'll recommend the best option for your driveway.
