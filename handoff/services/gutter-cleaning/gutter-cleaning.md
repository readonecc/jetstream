## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our gutter cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: How often should gutters be cleaned?
**A1**: In Brisbane, we recommend having your gutters cleaned at least twice a year — ideally before and after storm season. Properties surrounded by overhanging trees may need more frequent clearing to prevent blockages.

**Q2**: What happens if gutters are left blocked?
**A2**: Blocked gutters cause water to overflow and pool around your roofline, fascia, and foundation. Over time, this can lead to timber rot, mould growth, interior water damage, and costly structural repairs.

**Q3**: Do you clean both inside and outside the gutters?
**A3**: Yes. Our gutter cleaning service includes clearing leaves and debris from inside the gutter channels, flushing downpipes, and cleaning the outer gutter faces to remove mould, black streaking, and staining. You get a complete gutter clean inside and out.

**Q4**: Do you flush the downpipes as well?
**A4**: Yes. Every gutter clean includes a full downpipe flush to make sure water can drain freely from your gutter channels all the way through to the stormwater system.

**Q5**: Can you clean gutters on a two-storey home?
**A5**: Absolutely. Our team is equipped and trained to safely work at height on single and multi-storey properties. We carry all necessary safety equipment and follow strict height safety procedures on every job.
