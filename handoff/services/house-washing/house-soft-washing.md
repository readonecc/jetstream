## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our house soft washing service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: What is house soft washing?
**A1**: House soft washing uses specialised cleaning solutions and low pressure to safely remove mould, algae, dirt, and environmental buildup from your home's exterior. Unlike high-pressure washing, it's gentle enough for painted surfaces, window frames, and trims while still delivering a thorough clean.

**Q2**: Is soft washing safe for my paint and surfaces?
**A2**: Yes. Soft washing is specifically designed for delicate exterior surfaces. The low-pressure application won't strip paint, damage window seals, or harm exterior finishes. It's the method we recommend for most residential homes across Brisbane.

**Q3**: How often should I get my house soft washed?
**A3**: In Brisbane's climate, we recommend a soft wash every 12 to 18 months. Properties surrounded by trees or in high-humidity areas may benefit from more frequent cleaning to stay on top of mould and algae growth.

**Q4**: How long does a house soft wash take?
**A4**: Most residential homes take between 2 to 4 hours depending on the size of the property and the level of buildup. We'll give you a clear timeframe when we provide your quote.

**Q5**: Do I need to be home during the wash?
**A5**: Not necessarily. As long as we have access to the exterior of your home and water supply, we can complete the job while you're out. We'll walk you through the results when you're available and payment is only on completion.
