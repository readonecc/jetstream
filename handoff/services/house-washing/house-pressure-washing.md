## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our house pressure washing service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: What is house pressure washing?
**A1**: House pressure washing uses controlled high-pressure water to remove heavy dirt, grime, and surface staining from your home's durable exterior surfaces. It is best suited for surfaces that can handle higher pressure, such as brick, concrete, and rendered walls in solid condition.

**Q2**: Is pressure washing safe for my home's surfaces?
**A2**: Yes, when carried out by trained professionals. We assess every surface before we start and adjust the pressure accordingly. Durable materials like brick, stone, and concrete respond well to high-pressure cleaning. For more delicate surfaces like painted walls and trims, we recommend our soft washing service instead.

**Q3**: What is the difference between pressure washing and soft washing?
**A3**: Pressure washing uses controlled high-pressure water to remove heavy buildup from hard, durable surfaces. Soft washing uses low pressure combined with specialised cleaning solutions to safely clean delicate surfaces like painted walls, window frames, and trims. We will recommend the right method for your property during the quoting process.

**Q4**: How long does a house pressure wash take?
**A4**: Most residential homes take between 2 to 4 hours depending on the size of the property and the level of dirt and grime buildup. We will provide a clear timeframe when we send through your quote.

**Q5**: Do I need to be home during the pressure wash?
**A5**: Not necessarily. As long as we have access to the exterior of your home and a water supply, we can complete the job while you are out. We will walk you through the results when you are available, and payment is only on completion.
