## Section 5: Category Overview

**H2**: Why House Washing Matters for Brisbane Homes

Your home's exterior is constantly exposed to the elements. In Brisbane's subtropical climate — with high humidity, heavy rainfall, and warm temperatures year-round — mould, algae, dirt, and pollution build up faster than most homeowners realise. Left untreated, this buildup doesn't just look unsightly. It can damage paint, degrade render, stain surfaces permanently, and reduce your property's overall value.

Professional house washing is the most effective way to remove that buildup and protect your home's exterior for the long term. Unlike a quick hose-down, a proper house wash targets the root cause of discolouration and organic growth, leaving your walls clean and treated against rapid regrowth. It's one of the most affordable ways to maintain your property's appearance and keep it looking well cared for.

At Jetstream Pressure Washing AU, we offer two proven methods for house washing in Brisbane: soft washing and pressure washing. Soft washing is our go-to for most residential exteriors — it uses low pressure combined with professional-grade cleaning solutions to safely break down mould and grime without risking damage to painted surfaces, window seals, or trims. For harder, more durable surfaces like brick and exposed concrete, pressure washing provides the additional force needed to strip away heavy staining and restore the original finish.

Not sure which method is right for your home? That's what we're here for. As a local, family-owned business, we assess every property individually and recommend the approach that will deliver the best result for your specific surfaces. With hundreds of 5-star reviews, a 100% money-back guarantee, and payment only on completion, you can trust that your home is in good hands.

## Section 13: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our house washing services in Brisbane.

**Q1**: What's the difference between soft washing and pressure washing for a house?
**A1**: Soft washing uses low pressure and specialised cleaning solutions to safely remove mould, algae, and dirt from delicate surfaces like painted walls, rendered finishes, and weatherboard. Pressure washing uses controlled high pressure for harder, more durable surfaces like brick and concrete. We assess your home and recommend the right method for each surface — and many jobs use a combination of both.

**Q2**: How often should I have my house washed?
**A2**: In Brisbane's humid, subtropical climate, we recommend having your home's exterior washed every 12 to 24 months. Properties in shaded areas, near bushland, or in high-humidity zones may need more frequent washing to keep mould and algae under control.

**Q3**: Is house washing safe for all exterior surfaces?
**A3**: Yes. We tailor our approach to suit your home's specific materials. Soft washing is safe for painted surfaces, render, Colorbond cladding, weatherboard, and window trims. Pressure washing is reserved for durable surfaces that can handle higher pressure. We never use a one-size-fits-all approach.

**Q4**: Will house washing damage my paint or window seals?
**A4**: Not when it's done properly. Our team is trained to adjust pressure levels and nozzle types for every surface. Soft washing, in particular, is designed to clean painted and sealed surfaces without causing any damage. That's one of the reasons we offer a 100% money-back guarantee on every job.

**Q5**: How much does house washing cost in Brisbane?
**A5**: Every home is different, so we provide free, no-obligation quotes based on your property's size, surface types, and the level of buildup.
