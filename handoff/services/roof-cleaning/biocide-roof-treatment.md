## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our biocide roof treatment service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: What is a biocide roof treatment?
**A1**: A biocide roof treatment involves applying a professional-grade solution to your roof that kills existing mould, moss, algae, and lichen — and prevents them from growing back. The treatment penetrates organic growth and continues working over weeks and months after application.

**Q2**: How long does the biocide treatment last?
**A2**: Results vary depending on your roof's exposure to shade, moisture, and vegetation, but a professional biocide treatment typically provides protection for 12 months or longer. Regular reapplication keeps your roof protected year-round.

**Q3**: Do I need a roof clean before the biocide treatment?
**A3**: We recommend a soft wash before applying biocide for the best results. Removing the bulk of organic growth first allows the biocide solution to penetrate more effectively and provide longer-lasting protection. We can combine both services in a single visit.

**Q4**: Is biocide safe for my roof and surroundings?
**A4**: Yes. The professional-grade biocide products we use are designed specifically for exterior surfaces. They're safe for all common roofing materials including Colorbond, concrete tiles, and terracotta. We take care to protect surrounding plants and landscaping during application.

**Q5**: How soon will I see results?
**A5**: You'll notice initial results within the first few days as the biocide begins breaking down organic growth. Full results typically develop over 4 to 6 weeks as moss, algae, and lichen continue to deteriorate and wash away naturally with rainfall.
