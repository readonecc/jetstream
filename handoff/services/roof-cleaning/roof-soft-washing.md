## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our roof soft washing service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: What is roof soft washing?
**A1**: Roof soft washing uses specialised cleaning solutions and low-pressure application to safely remove mould, algae, moss, and lichen from your roof. Unlike high-pressure methods, soft washing treats organic growth at the source without risking damage to roofing materials, tiles, or coatings.

**Q2**: Is soft washing safe for my roof type?
**A2**: Yes. Soft washing is safe for virtually all roofing types including Colorbond, concrete tiles, terracotta tiles, and metal roofing. The low-pressure method ensures no damage to surfaces, coatings, or fixings. It's the approach we recommend for most residential roofs across Brisbane.

**Q3**: How often should I get my roof cleaned?
**A3**: In Brisbane's climate, we recommend a roof soft wash every 12 to 24 months depending on your roof's exposure to shade, trees, and moisture. Properties in high-humidity areas or surrounded by vegetation may benefit from more frequent cleaning.

**Q4**: How long does a roof soft wash take?
**A4**: Most residential roofs take between 2 to 4 hours depending on the size of the roof and the level of organic growth. We'll provide a clear timeframe when we send through your quote.

**Q5**: Will roof soft washing remove black streaks and staining?
**A5**: Yes. Black streaks on roofs are typically caused by algae growth. Our soft wash solution breaks down the algae and removes the staining, restoring your roof's original appearance. For stubborn staining, we may recommend a biocide treatment to ensure long-lasting results.
