## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our roof pressure washing service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: What is roof pressure washing?
**A1**: Roof pressure washing uses controlled high-pressure water to remove heavy dirt, grime, environmental staining, and years of buildup from durable roofing surfaces. It's an effective method for roofs that need more than a soft wash to restore their appearance.

**Q2**: Is high pressure safe for my roof?
**A2**: High-pressure cleaning is safe for durable roofing materials such as concrete tiles and certain metal roofing. We assess every roof before we start and adjust the pressure to suit the surface. For more delicate roofing materials, we recommend our soft washing service instead.

**Q3**: When should I choose pressure washing over soft washing?
**A3**: Pressure washing is ideal when your roof has heavy dirt buildup, stubborn environmental staining, or years of accumulated grime that low-pressure methods can't effectively remove. We'll assess your roof and recommend the best approach during your free quote.

**Q4**: How long does a roof pressure wash take?
**A4**: Most residential roofs take between 2 to 4 hours depending on the size of the roof and the severity of the buildup. We'll give you a clear timeframe when we provide your quote.

**Q5**: Do you clean gutters as part of the service?
**A5**: Roof pressure washing focuses on the roof surfaces themselves. If your gutters need attention, we offer a dedicated gutter cleaning service that can be booked alongside your roof clean for a complete result.
