## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our retaining wall cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: Can you clean all types of retaining walls?
**A1**: Yes. We clean concrete block, timber, stone, and rendered retaining walls. Our team adjusts the pressure and technique to suit the material so your wall is cleaned thoroughly without any damage.

**Q2**: Will pressure washing damage the wall?
**A2**: No. We use the appropriate pressure settings for each wall type and material. Our team is experienced in cleaning retaining walls safely, ensuring a thorough result without compromising the structure or finish.

**Q3**: How often should retaining walls be cleaned?
**A3**: In Brisbane's climate, we recommend cleaning retaining walls every 12 to 24 months depending on their exposure to moisture and shade. Walls near garden beds or in damp areas may need more frequent attention.

**Q4**: Do you clean garden walls too?
**A4**: Absolutely. We clean garden walls, boundary walls, and feature walls in addition to retaining walls. If it's an exterior wall surface, we can clean it.

**Q5**: Can you remove green algae staining?
**A5**: Yes. Green algae is one of the most common issues we treat on retaining walls across Brisbane. Our pressure cleaning process removes algae staining effectively, restoring the wall's original appearance.
