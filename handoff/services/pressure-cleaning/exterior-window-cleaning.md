## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our exterior window cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: Do you clean window frames as well?
**A1**: Yes. Our exterior window cleaning service includes the glass, frames, trims, and sills. We clean the full window area to ensure a consistent, thorough result.

**Q2**: How often should exterior windows be cleaned?
**A2**: We recommend having your exterior windows professionally cleaned every 6 to 12 months. Properties near busy roads or construction sites may benefit from more frequent cleaning due to higher dust and debris exposure.

**Q3**: Can you clean second-storey windows?
**A3**: Yes. We have the equipment and experience to safely clean windows on multi-storey properties. We'll assess access requirements when we provide your quote.

**Q4**: Is it safe for tinted windows?
**A4**: Absolutely. We use cleaning methods and products that are safe for tinted and coated window glass. Our team takes care to protect all window surfaces during the cleaning process.

**Q5**: Do you clean both residential and commercial windows?
**A5**: We do. We service residential homes, commercial buildings, and retail shopfronts across Brisbane. Whether it's a handful of windows or an entire building facade, we deliver the same streak-free standard on every job.
