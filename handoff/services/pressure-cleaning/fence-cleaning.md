## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our fence cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: Can you clean Colorbond fences?
**A1**: Yes. We clean Colorbond, timber, aluminium, and rendered fences. Our team adjusts the pressure and technique to suit the material, ensuring a thorough clean without damaging the surface or finish.

**Q2**: Is pressure washing safe for timber fences?
**A2**: Absolutely. We use appropriate pressure settings for timber fences to remove mould and grime without damaging the wood. Our team is experienced in cleaning all types of timber fencing safely.

**Q3**: How long does fence cleaning take?
**A3**: Most residential fence cleaning jobs take between 1 to 3 hours depending on the length and condition of the fence. We'll give you a clear timeframe when we provide your quote.

**Q4**: Do you clean both sides of the fence?
**A4**: We clean the side or sides that are accessible from your property. If you need both sides cleaned and have access, we're happy to include that in the job.

**Q5**: How often should fences be cleaned?
**A5**: We recommend having your fences professionally cleaned every 12 to 24 months in Brisbane's climate. Fences that are heavily shaded or near gardens tend to collect mould and grime faster and may benefit from more frequent cleaning.
