## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our patio pressure cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: Can you clean covered patios?
**A1**: Yes. We clean both open and covered patio areas. Covered patios still collect mould, dust, and grime over time, and our equipment is well suited to cleaning these spaces thoroughly.

**Q2**: Will pressure washing damage my patio?
**A2**: No. We use the correct pressure settings and techniques for your specific surface type. Whether your patio is concrete, tiled, or paved, we tailor our approach to deliver a deep clean without causing any damage.

**Q3**: How long does patio cleaning take?
**A3**: Most residential patios take between 1 to 2 hours depending on the size and condition. We'll provide a clear timeframe when we send through your quote.

**Q4**: How often should I get my patio cleaned?
**A4**: We recommend a professional patio clean every 12 to 18 months in Brisbane's climate. Properties with heavy tree cover or limited airflow may benefit from more frequent cleaning.

**Q5**: Do you clean patio furniture too?
**A5**: Our service focuses on patio floors and hard surfaces. While we don't specifically clean outdoor furniture, we take care to protect any items in the area during the cleaning process.
