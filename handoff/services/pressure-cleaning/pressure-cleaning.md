## Section 13: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our pressure cleaning services in Brisbane.

**Q1**: What surfaces can you pressure clean?
**A1**: We pressure clean driveways, pathways, patios, decks, pool areas, retaining walls, fences, exterior windows, and solar panels. We work with concrete, pavers, exposed aggregate, natural stone, timber, tiles, Colorbond, brick, rendered surfaces, and more. Whatever the surface, we match our equipment and technique to deliver the best result without damage.

**Q2**: How often should exterior surfaces be pressure cleaned?
**A2**: In Brisbane's climate, most exterior surfaces benefit from a professional pressure clean every 12 to 18 months. High-traffic areas like driveways and pathways, or surfaces in shaded, moisture-prone spots, may need cleaning more frequently to prevent mould and algae from taking hold.

**Q3**: Will pressure cleaning damage my surfaces?
**A3**: Not when it's done properly. We adjust pressure levels, nozzle types, and cleaning techniques to suit each surface. Tough surfaces like concrete get high-pressure treatment, while delicate surfaces like timber decks, painted fences, and solar panels are cleaned at lower pressures with specialised methods. Our team is trained to get the best result without causing any damage.

**Q4**: How long does a pressure cleaning job take?
**A4**: It depends on the size and condition of the area. A standard driveway typically takes one to two hours. Larger jobs or multiple surfaces may take half a day or longer. We'll give you a clear timeframe when we provide your free quote so you know exactly what to expect.

**Q5**: Do you service both residential and commercial properties?
**A5**: Yes. We clean residential homes, townhouses, and investment properties, as well as commercial sites including warehouses, car parks, retail shopfronts, and body corporate common areas. No matter the size of the job, we bring the right equipment and deliver the same standard of work.
