## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our solar panel cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: How often should solar panels be cleaned?
**A1**: We recommend having your solar panels professionally cleaned every 12 months. Properties near busy roads, construction sites, or areas with high bird activity may benefit from more frequent cleaning to maintain peak performance.

**Q2**: Will cleaning improve my energy output?
**A2**: Yes. Removing dirt, dust, and bird droppings from your panels allows more sunlight to reach the cells, which directly improves energy production. Many customers notice a measurable difference in output after a professional clean.

**Q3**: Is it safe to pressure wash solar panels?
**A3**: We don't use high-pressure methods on solar panels. Our cleaning process uses gentle techniques and purified water to safely remove buildup without scratching or damaging the panel surface or seals.

**Q4**: Do you clean commercial solar systems?
**A4**: Yes. We clean residential and commercial solar panel systems across Brisbane. Whether it's a small rooftop array or a large commercial installation, we have the equipment and experience to handle it.

**Q5**: Can you clean panels on a steep roof?
**A5**: We can. Our team is experienced in working on a range of roof types and pitches. We assess access and safety requirements before every job to ensure the work is completed safely and effectively.
