## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our deck pressure cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: Is pressure washing safe for timber decks?
**A1**: Yes. We adjust the pressure settings and use the correct nozzles to clean timber decks thoroughly without damaging the wood grain or surface. Our team has experience with all types of timber and composite decking.

**Q2**: How often should I clean my deck?
**A2**: In Brisbane's climate, we recommend having your deck professionally cleaned every 12 months. Decks that are heavily shaded or exposed to moisture may benefit from more frequent cleaning to prevent mould and algae buildup.

**Q3**: Can you remove algae from decking?
**A3**: Absolutely. Algae is one of the most common problems we deal with on decks across Brisbane. Our pressure cleaning process removes algae growth thoroughly, restoring grip and improving the overall appearance of the surface.

**Q4**: Do you clean raised decks?
**A4**: Yes. We clean raised decks, elevated entertaining areas, and multi-level deck structures. Our equipment allows us to work across a range of deck heights and configurations.

**Q5**: Should I seal my deck after cleaning?
**A5**: Sealing after cleaning is a great way to protect your deck from moisture, UV damage, and future organic growth. While we focus on pressure cleaning, we're happy to advise on the best next steps for protecting your deck.
