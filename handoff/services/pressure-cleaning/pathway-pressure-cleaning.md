## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our pathway pressure cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: Do you clean garden paths?
**A1**: Yes. We clean all types of pathways including garden paths, entry walkways, side access paths, and shared access walkways. Our equipment is suited to a range of surfaces and pathway sizes.

**Q2**: Can you remove algae from pathways?
**A2**: Absolutely. Algae is one of the most common issues we treat on pathways across Brisbane. Our high-pressure cleaning removes algae growth thoroughly, restoring grip and reducing the risk of slips and falls.

**Q3**: How often should pathways be cleaned?
**A3**: We recommend having your pathways professionally cleaned every 12 to 18 months. Properties in shaded or high-moisture areas may benefit from more frequent cleaning to prevent algae and mould from building up.

**Q4**: Is pressure washing safe for pavers?
**A4**: Yes. We adjust our pressure settings and techniques to suit the surface type. Paved pathways are cleaned thoroughly without dislodging jointing sand or damaging the pavers themselves.

**Q5**: Do you service commercial pathways?
**A5**: We do. We clean pathways for commercial properties, apartment complexes, schools, and public facilities. Whether it's a single walkway or an entire site, we have the equipment and experience to handle it.
