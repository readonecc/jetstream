## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our concrete cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: What types of concrete surfaces do you clean?
**A1**: We clean all types of concrete surfaces including driveways, pathways, patios, pool surrounds, garage floors, retaining walls, and car parks. Whether it's exposed aggregate, plain concrete, or stamped concrete, we have the equipment and experience to deliver outstanding results.

**Q2**: Can you remove oil stains from concrete?
**A2**: In most cases, yes. We pre-treat oil stains with specialised cleaning solutions before pressure washing. Fresh stains typically come out completely, while older or deeply set stains may lighten significantly. We'll always be upfront about what to expect during the quoting process.

**Q3**: How often should concrete be professionally cleaned?
**A3**: In Brisbane's climate, we recommend having your concrete surfaces professionally cleaned every 12 to 18 months. High-traffic areas or surfaces in shaded, damp locations may benefit from more frequent cleaning to prevent mould and algae buildup.

**Q4**: Will pressure washing damage my concrete?
**A4**: No. Our team uses the correct pressure settings and professional-grade surface cleaning equipment for each job. We adjust our approach based on the type and condition of your concrete to deliver a thorough clean without causing damage.

**Q5**: Do you clean concrete around pools?
**A5**: Absolutely. Pool surrounds are one of the most common concrete surfaces we clean. We remove mould, algae, and slip hazards to keep your pool area safe and looking great. We use cleaning solutions that are safe for use around pool water.
