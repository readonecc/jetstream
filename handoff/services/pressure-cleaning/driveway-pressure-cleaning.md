## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our driveway pressure cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: Can you remove oil stains from my driveway?
**A1**: In most cases, yes. We pre-treat oil stains with specialised cleaning solutions before pressure washing. While fresh stains typically come out completely, older or deeply set stains may lighten significantly rather than disappear entirely. We'll always be upfront about what to expect.

**Q2**: How long does driveway pressure cleaning take?
**A2**: Most standard residential driveways take between 1 to 3 hours depending on the size and level of buildup. We'll give you a clear timeframe when we provide your quote so you know what to expect.

**Q3**: How often should I clean my driveway?
**A3**: In Brisbane's climate, we recommend having your driveway professionally cleaned every 12 to 18 months. Properties under heavy tree cover or in shaded areas may need more frequent cleaning to stay on top of mould and algae growth.

**Q4**: Will pressure washing damage my driveway?
**A4**: No. Our team uses the correct pressure settings and equipment for your specific surface type. Whether it's concrete, pavers, or exposed aggregate, we adjust our approach to deliver a thorough clean without causing damage.

**Q5**: Do you clean paved driveways too?
**A5**: Absolutely. We clean all types of driveways including concrete, paved, and shared access driveways. Our equipment and techniques are tailored to suit each surface so you get the best result every time.
