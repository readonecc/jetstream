## Section 14: FAQ Section

- **Kicker**: GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2**: Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle**: Common questions about our pool area pressure cleaning service in Brisbane.

**Q1**: Is pressure washing safe around my pool?
**A1**: Yes. We take care to direct water and debris away from the pool during cleaning. Our team is experienced in working around pool areas and takes every precaution to protect your pool water and surrounds.

**Q2**: Can you remove algae from pool surrounds?
**A2**: Absolutely. Algae buildup on pool surrounds is one of the most common jobs we handle. Our high-pressure cleaning removes algae thoroughly, restoring grip and reducing the risk of slips around the pool.

**Q3**: How often should pool areas be cleaned?
**A3**: We recommend having your pool area professionally cleaned every 12 months. Pool surrounds that are heavily shaded or constantly wet may need more frequent attention to stay safe and clean.

**Q4**: Do you use chemicals near the pool?
**A4**: We use professional-grade cleaning solutions only where necessary, and we take care to keep any products away from pool water. Our team follows safe practices to protect your pool and surrounding areas.

**Q5**: Do you clean pool fencing too?
**A5**: Yes. We can clean pool fencing as part of your pool area service. Glass, aluminium, and tubular fencing all collect grime and water spots, and a professional clean makes a noticeable difference.
