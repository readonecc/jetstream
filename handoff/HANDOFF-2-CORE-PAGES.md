- **Kicker:** GOT QUESTIONS?
- **H2:** Frequently Asked Questions
- **Subtitle:** Got a question about our services? Here are the answers to the most common ones. If you need more info, give us a call.

**Q1:** What types of surfaces do you clean?
**A1:** We clean just about every exterior surface — house walls, roofs, driveways, pathways, patios, decks, gutters, fences, retaining walls, pool areas, and more. We also service commercial properties including warehouses, car parks, loading docks, retail shopfronts, and body corporate common areas across Brisbane.

**Q2:** What's the difference between soft washing and pressure washing?
**A2:** Soft washing uses low pressure combined with specialised cleaning solutions to safely remove mould, algae, and grime from delicate surfaces like painted walls and roofs. Pressure washing uses controlled high pressure for tougher surfaces like concrete driveways and pathways. We assess every job and use the right method for the surface.

**Q3:** Are you licensed and insured?
**A3:** Yes. We're fully licensed and carry $20 million in public liability insurance. We provide all relevant documentation upfront with every quote so you have complete peace of mind before we start.

**Q5:** Do you offer any discounts?

**A6:** We service all of Brisbane and surrounding areas including Ipswich, Logan, Redland, Moreton Bay, Caboolture, Redcliffe, Springfield, and more. If you're unsure whether we cover your area, give us a call and we'll let you know.
